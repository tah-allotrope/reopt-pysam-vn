4. Ecoplexus Sharing  Cancelling the Ccl (Charges for Difference Compensation regarding EVN's payments to
                      other backup sources) and fixing a cap on DPPA fee variation per year;
      Proposes and
  Recommendations     Aggregation mechanism for clean electricity instead of the current 30-minute matching
                      mechanism; Such as:
                      1/ Aggregation of supply: surplus electricity needs to be stored as renewable energy
                      credits and sold to Large Customers during night hours.
                      2/ Aggregation of demand: electricity buyers need to collect many electricity meters in
                      the same location to buy electricity from a large project

                      Bidding for investor selection criteria on lowest price is conflicting with the priorities that
                      DPPA projects are striving for (more contribution to the tax);

                      Risks & CfD Contract: Negotiations on CfD Contract need to consider through on such
                      as the absence of payment guarantees, unilateral termination of the contract, and
                      uncertainties regarding the contract term;
