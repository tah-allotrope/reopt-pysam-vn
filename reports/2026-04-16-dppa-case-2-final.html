05 Math · Algorithm Used

The DPPA Case 2 settlement uses a synthetic/financial model with the following key calculations:

Settlement Quantity Rule: min(buyer_load_kWh, contracted_generation_kWh) — buyer only pays for the lesser of actual load or available generation.

Buyer Premium: (strike_price_vnd - market_price_vnd) × settlement_quantity_kWh — difference between contract strike and market reference, applied to settled quantity.

Developer NPV: Σ(cash_flow_t / (1+irr)^t) — equity returns discounted at project IRR, evaluated against developer hurdle rate.

CFD Stress: sensitivity to market price volatility under stressed conditions (20% price drop scenario).
