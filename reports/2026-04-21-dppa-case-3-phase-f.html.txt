H2: Limitations / Second-Best Alternative
P: Why this happened: At the buyer-optimized strike (5% below EVN), the developer's total PPA rate of 2,334 VND/kWh yields ~479K USD/yr in revenue. The project's 70% debt financing (sized at $5.3M by PySAM's IRR-targeting algorithm) requires ~$810K/yr in debt service — a 41% shortfall before O&M. The negative DSCR (-0.175) confirms structural insolvency.
P: Second-best path: Raise the strike above the 5% discount threshold. The developer's rate needs to reach approximately 3,900+ VND/kWh (≈ $0.153/kWh) at current capital costs to achieve a 1.0 DSCR at 70% leverage. This would increase buyer cost but unlock developer financing. Alternatively, increase project size to spread fixed costs over more generation.
P: Note: The bounded-opt solution is at the buyer's cost minimum — Phase F reveals the developer cannot finance at that price. This is not a model failure; it is the correct output of the Phase F gate.
