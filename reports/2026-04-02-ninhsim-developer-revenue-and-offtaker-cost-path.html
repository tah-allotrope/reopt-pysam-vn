04 / Results & Visualizations

Results & Visualizations

The added screening view shows that, under the fixed-volume escalated-price replay, annual developer revenue rises from about $10.66M in year 1 to $26.93M in year 20. Customer total cost stays essentially equal to the escalated EVN benchmark by construction, so the modeled customer savings remain near zero in NPV terms.

05 / Open Questions / Next Phase Seeds

Open Questions / Next Phase Seeds

  • This screening view keeps delivered energy fixed across all years. The next stronger phase would introduce degradation, load growth, or changing residual EVN exposure.
  • The current developer-revenue view assumes all delivered renewable energy earns the bundled strike. If the commercial structure needs merchant handling for excess renewable generation or curtailment risk, that should become the next explicit enhancement.
  • The modeled offtaker savings stay effectively zero because the strike path is set to match the EVN benchmark. A decision-oriented next step would add below-benchmark or above-benchmark sensitivity bands around that ceiling.
