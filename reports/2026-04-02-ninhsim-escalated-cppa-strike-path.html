05 / Open Questions / Next Phase Seeds

  • This pass escalates prices only; the delivery mix and residual grid share remain fixed at the solved year-one profile. A richer next phase would model volume drift, degradation, or tariff-shape changes over time.
  • The current benchmark path assumes the residual EVN unit price escalates at the same 5% annual rate as the weighted EVN benchmark. If the study needs separate TOU-component escalation, that should become the next pricing refinement.
