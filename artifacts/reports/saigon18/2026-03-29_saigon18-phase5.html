Phase 5 — Decree 146/2025 Two-Part Tariff Sensitivity

Decree 146/2025 pilots a capacity charge (VND/kW-month) for industrial customers (Jan–Jun 2026). A capacity charge rewards assets that reduce peak demand. Saigon18's solar+BESS reduces the annual grid-import peak from 30,246 kW (BAU) to 27,104 kW (current REopt dispatch) — a 3,142 kW reduction. With demand-shaving optimisation the estimated achievable peak is 24,598 kW (additional 2,506 kW via BESS dispatch re-tuning).
