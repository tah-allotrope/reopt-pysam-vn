Script Bugs Identified in Phase 2

Bug 1 — equity_irr.py: EBITDA uses discounted NPV, not nominal annual savings

Root cause: extract_annual_ebitda() computes lcc_bau − lcc_opt which equals the NPV (both LCCs are present-value lifecycle costs). Dividing this discounted total by a nominal escalation-factor sum yields year-1 EBITDA of ~$319K instead of ~$5.93M — a 18.6x understatement.

Effect: Equity cash flows in years 1–10 average −$4.7M/yr. IRR converges to −17.9%. This is a pure model artifact and does not reflect project economics.

Fix: Replace LCC-diff logic with Financial.year_one_total_operating_cost_savings_before_tax as the year-1 EBITDA base, escalated at elec_cost_escalation_rate_fraction.

Bug 2 — compare_reopt_vs_excel.py: Energy flow keys don't exist in REopt output

Root cause: The load_reopt_metrics() function references key names that do not appear in the REopt results JSON. All energy-flow metrics silently resolve to 0.
